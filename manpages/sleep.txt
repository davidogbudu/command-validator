NAME
    sleep - delay for a specified amount of time

DESCRIPTION
    The sleep command delays for a specified amount of time.

SYNOPSIS
    sleep [TIME]

OPTIONS
    -s, --seconds            TIME is in seconds (default)
    -m, --minutes            TIME is in minutes
    -h, --hours              TIME is in hours
    -d, --days               TIME is in days

EXAMPLE
    sleep 10
    sleep 5m
